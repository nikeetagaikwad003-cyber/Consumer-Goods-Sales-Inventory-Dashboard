# Consumer Goods Sales & Inventory Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes consumer goods sales and inventory data using Power BI. The dashboard helps track sales performance, inventory levels, and stock efficiency, enabling better inventory planning and demand forecasting.

The project simulates a real-world retail scenario where sales and inventory must be monitored together.

---

##  Business Objectives
- Monitor total sales and inventory levels
- Identify fast-moving and slow-moving products
- Compare sales performance with available stock
- Reduce stock-outs and overstock situations

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- Data Modeling
- DAX Measures
- Interactive Dashboards

---

##  Key Metrics & Analysis
- Total Sales Amount
- Units Sold by Product Category
- Inventory Quantity by Product
- Sales vs Inventory Comparison
- Stock Turnover Insights

---

##  Dashboard Features
- Interactive slicers for:
  - Product Category
  - Time Period
- Sales and inventory comparison charts
- Highlighting low-stock and high-demand products
- Clean and professional layout
